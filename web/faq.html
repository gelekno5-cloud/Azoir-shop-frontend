Getting started

How do I start a commission?

Begin with a short conversation — tell us the occasion, the person, a stone you love, or simply a feeling. A designer replies within one business day. The first consultation is free, in person in New York or by video from anywhere, with no obligation.

Do I need to know exactly what I want?

Not at all. Most people arrive with a feeling rather than a finished idea — a photo, an heirloom, a colour, a memory. Our job is to listen and shape it into something real. There are no templates and no pressure to decide.

Do you work with clients outside New York?

Yes. We craft every piece in New York, but we work with clients anywhere in the world by video and email. The only difference is that we ship your finished piece to you, fully insured, rather than hand it over in person.

Design & process

What does the design process look like?

After our first conversation, our designers develop three distinct concepts, then bring your favourite to life as a photorealistic 3D render. You refine the details over up to two rounds of revisions, we run a production feasibility review, and nothing is cast until you approve the final design. See the full walkthrough on How it works.

Will I see the piece before it's made?

Always. You'll see your design as a professional CAD render — from every angle, at true scale — long before any metal is touched. You approve exactly what you'll receive.

How many revisions do I get?

Up to two rounds of revisions on your chosen concept, plus a feasibility review to make sure it will wear and last beautifully. We'd rather take an extra pass than let a detail slip.

Can you redesign an heirloom or reuse my stones?

Yes — heirloom redesign is one of our favourite things to do. We can reset your existing stones or rework inherited metal into something you'll actually wear, while keeping the story intact.

Pricing & payment

How much does a piece cost?

Because every piece is one of a kind, there's no set price list. Once your design is defined we send a detailed quote, so you know the full cost before committing to production. Your budget is part of the conversation from the very start — we design to it, not around it.

How does payment work?

In clear milestones: a design fee to begin the creative work, a deposit before production, and the balance before delivery. Every amount is agreed up front — no surprises. Payments are made securely by card through a private link.

What is the design fee?

It reserves your designer's time to create your three concepts and photorealistic render — the creative work that turns an idea into something buildable. It's agreed before we begin, so you're never charged for anything you didn't approve.

Materials & sourcing

What metals and stones do you work with?

Sterling silver, 10–24K gold in yellow, white and rose, and platinum — set with natural or lab-grown diamonds and a full range of coloured gemstones. If you have something specific in mind, we'll source it. More detail in our Materials guide.

Lab-grown or natural diamonds?

Both. Lab-grown and natural diamonds are chemically and optically identical; the difference is origin and price. We'll walk you through the trade-offs and you approve every stone before it's set.

Are your materials ethically sourced?

Yes. We work with conflict-free diamonds and responsibly sourced gemstones and metals, and we're always happy to talk about where a stone comes from.

Timing, delivery & care

How long does a commission take?

It depends on the piece and its complexity. Most commissions take a few weeks from approved design to delivery; your exact timeline is shared with your quote. If you're working toward a specific date, tell us early and we'll plan around it.

How is my piece delivered?

Fully insured and tracked, in our signature Azoir packaging, with its own certificate. Shipping within the continental U.S. is complimentary; we're happy to quote insured international delivery. Full detail on Shipping & Returns.

What if the ring doesn't fit, or I need a return?

Rings are made to the size you confirm before production, and many adjustments after delivery are straightforward — just reach out. Because each piece is custom-made, commissions are generally not returnable, but your happiness is the point: if a finished piece doesn't match the design you approved or arrives with a fault, we'll make it right. See our guarantee.

How do I look after it?

Fine jewelry lasts generations with a little care. We've put together simple guidance on cleaning, storage and wear in our Care & maintenance guide.
