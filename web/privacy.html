Privacy Policy

Last updated · July 2026

This policy explains what information we collect when you use azoir.co and how we use it. We keep it simple: we only collect what we need to respond to you, and we never sell your data.

What we collect

When you submit a commission inquiry, we collect the details you provide — your name, email, phone (optional), and anything you tell us about the piece you're imagining, including any inspiration images you choose to share.

How we use it

We use your information solely to respond to your inquiry, prepare a quote, and carry out your commission. That's it.

Analytics & cookies

With your consent, we use analytics cookies to understand how the site is used. You can decline these from the banner, and analytics will not load. Essential functionality never requires cookies.

Who we share it with

We do not sell or rent your information. We use a small number of trusted service providers to operate — for example, an email delivery provider and a hosting provider. They process data only on our behalf.

Retention & your rights

We keep inquiry details for as long as needed to serve you. You can ask us to access, correct, or delete your information at any time.
