How it works

Four steps, one perfect piece.

It begins with a conversation. From there we sketch, design, review, and refine — until it's exactly right.

01

Style exploration

Share your inspiration, your materials, your story. We listen first — no templates, no pressure.

02

Concepts & 3D

Three design concepts, then a professional CAD render so you see your piece from every angle.

03

Details, perfected

Up to two rounds of revisions and a feasibility review. You approve the final design before we begin.

04

Crafted by hand

Cast, set, and finished in New York. Delivered insured, in our signature packaging.
