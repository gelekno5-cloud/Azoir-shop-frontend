How it works

From a first idea
to a piece you'll keep forever.

Every commission is a collaboration. Here's exactly what to expect — no surprises, no pressure, at every step.

01

Style exploration

It begins with a conversation. Tell us the occasion, the person, a stone you love, or simply a feeling. Meet us in person in New York, or by video from anywhere. We listen first — there are no templates and no pressure to decide.

02

Three concepts, then 3D

Our designers develop three distinct concepts, then bring your favourite to life in a professional CAD render. You'll see it from every angle, at true scale, long before any metal is touched — so you can fall in love with it first.

03

Details, perfected

Refine the metal, the setting, the finish, the stones — up to two rounds of revisions, plus a production feasibility review. Nothing moves forward until every detail is exactly as you want it, and you approve the final design.

04

Crafted & delivered

Cast, set by hand, and finished in New York by experienced artisans. Your piece arrives insured, in our signature Azoir packaging, with its own certificate — ready for the moment it was made for.
