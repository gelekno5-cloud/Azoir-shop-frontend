Accessibility

Last updated · July 2026

We want azoir.co to be usable and welcoming to everyone, including people who rely on assistive technology.

Our commitment

We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA, and we treat accessibility as an ongoing effort rather than a one-time task.

What we do

  • Meaningful text alternatives for images and clear, descriptive links
  • Sufficient colour contrast and readable type
  • Keyboard-navigable pages and sensible heading structure

Get in touch

If you encounter any barrier on this site, or need information in a different format, please let us know and we'll help directly.
